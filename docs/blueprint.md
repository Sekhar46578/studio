# **App Name**: ShopStock

## Core Features:

- Product Management: Add new products with details like name, description, price, and initial stock level. Default list of groceries in Indian stores.
- Stock Adjustment: Easily update stock levels to account for new deliveries, sales, or spoilage.
- Sales Recording: Record daily sales transactions, linking them to specific products.
- Sales History: View sales history by day, week, month, or custom date range. See total sales and best-selling products.
- Inventory Overview: Display real-time inventory levels for all products with alerts for low stock items.
- Trend Analysis: AI tool helps analyze sales trends and recommend optimal stock levels. The tool will analyze past sales and will optionally notify you when you may wish to modify ordering plans, pricing or other sales parameters.
- Account management: Allow users to sign-in and sign-out securely using the credentials saved on Firestore
- Multiple Language Options: Support multiple languages including Telugu, Hindi, and English.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Professional blue (#3498DB) for trust and efficiency.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F0F0) for a clean, modern look.
- Accent color: Darker blue (#2980B9) to highlight key actions and information.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, for a modern and neutral look.
- Use simple, clear icons to represent product categories and actions.
- Maintain a clean, uncluttered layout with a clear hierarchy of information.
- Subtle animations to confirm actions and provide feedback without being distracting.
